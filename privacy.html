Privacy Policy

Saif Social

Saif Social ("we" or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Saif Social collects:

  • Your email address and name
  • OAuth tokens from connected social accounts (TikTok, etc.)
  • Scheduled content and media files you upload

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to provide the service: scheduling and publishing your content to your connected social media accounts.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as necessary to publish content to your connected social media platforms.

Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. OAuth tokens are stored securely and never shared.

Your Rights

You may delete your account at any time, which removes your data within 30 days. You can disconnect any social media account at any time.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact Saif Social through the application.
