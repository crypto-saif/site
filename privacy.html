Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Postiz collects the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture
  • Social Media Data: OAuth tokens and profile information from connected social media accounts
  • Content: Posts, media files, and scheduling information you create
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the service, pages viewed, features used
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our service
  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Send you notifications about your scheduled posts
  • Analyze usage to improve user experience
  • Protect against fraud and abuse

3. Third-Party Services

Postiz integrates with third-party social media platforms. When you connect an account:

  • We receive OAuth tokens and basic profile information from that platform
  • We use this data only to perform actions you request (posting, scheduling, analytics)
  • Each platform's privacy policy applies to data they share with us

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage of OAuth tokens and credentials
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Your personal information is deleted within 30 days
  • OAuth tokens are immediately revoked
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Disconnect social media integrations at any time
  • Export your data

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Postiz is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the application or email.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us through our application.
